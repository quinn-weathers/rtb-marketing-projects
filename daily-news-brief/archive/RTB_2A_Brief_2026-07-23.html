No new reporting reached this run. No new reporting reached this run. As last verified, Gun Owners of America, Gun Owners Foundation, and Florida resident Sean Dale Henry filed Henry v. Brooks on July 20 in the U.S. District Court for the Virgin Islands, challenging a licensing scheme that, per the complaint, gives ordinary American visitors no lawful path to possess or carry a defensive handgun: the territory requires a license, recognizes no out-of-state permits, generally bars nonresidents from applying, prohibits open carry, and imposes a ten-year mandatory minimum (14 V.I.C. §2253) for unauthorized possession.
