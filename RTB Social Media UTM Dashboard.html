UTM Best Practices

Lowercase, always. GA4 treats Facebook and facebook as two different sources. This tool forces lowercase automatically.
Hyphens, not spaces. Spaces become %20 and split your reports. Use spring-membership-2026.
Never tag internal links. Only tag links that bring people to our site from outside (social, email, ads). Tagging links between our own pages breaks attribution.
