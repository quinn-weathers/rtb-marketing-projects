Christian v. James — 2nd Cir. strikes NY Vampire Rule (decided May 18; surfaced Thursday)

Most significant federal-circuit Second Amendment ruling since the VA signing. Judge Joseph Bianco (Trump appointee) wrote for a unanimous panel on the private-property-open-to-public question, joined by Judge Eunice Lee (Biden appointee); Judge Steven Menashi (Trump appointee) dissented on the parks question only. Plaintiffs SAF and FPC originally obtained the permanent injunction from US District Judge John Sinatra in October 2024. The ruling extends a near-unblemished post-Bruen winning streak against private-property carry presumptions, and lands while SCOTUS is actively considering Hawaii's near-identical Vampire Rule under Wolford v. Lopez. Note: not scored in the Mon-Wed window because it was published 6:09pm ET Monday on The Reload and surfaced only on the Thursday refresh — peak signal will land in next week's data.
